TRAVEL TRIVIA

Let's Play!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
To win the game, you must match the total score to the random number provided. If your total score goes above that of the random number, you lose.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on each image.
Each time the game starts, the crystals will be assigned new values to keep things interesting! Have fun and Good luck!
